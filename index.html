About The Elysian Library

The Elysian Library is a dedicated study space exclusively for female students. It provides a calm, peaceful, and secure environment for university, college, and medical entrance exam students who want to focus on their studies without distractions.

Located near major educational institutions in Quetta, the library is designed to support serious students seeking a quiet and comfortable place to learn.

Facilities

  • Peaceful and secure study environment
  • Seating capacity for 25 students
  • Comfortable study carrels
  • Individual lighting and power switches
  • High-speed complimentary Wi-Fi
  • UPS electricity backup
  • Dedicated prayer area
  • Rooftop terrace for open-air breaks

Location

Railway Cooperative Housing Society
Hazara Town Road Brewery
Quetta, Pakistan

Nearby institutions include Bolan Medical College, Bolan University of Health Sciences, SBK University, Hazara Town, Faisal Town and A-One City.
